Characters

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is the series' protagonist and possesses the ability to transform into a Titan. His intense desire for revenge against the Titans drives much of the plot.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman is Eren's adoptive sister and a highly skilled and stoic soldier. She is fiercely protective of Eren and possesses an innate sense of duty and loyalty.

Armin Arlert

Armin Arlert is Eren's childhood friend known for his intelligence and strategic thinking. He plays a crucial role in devising plans to combat the Titans.
